About

Why we are building it?

The pressing need to digitize and preserve the national language of Bhutan

We are motivated to bridge this gap and empower the Dzongkha-speaking community by enabling the recognition of handwritten Dzongkha text through an efficient and accurate OCR system.

Technology Used

In Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) it is used image recognition and processing tasks.
In Recurrent Neural Network(RNN) the output from the previous step is fed as input to the current step.
